# 🍽️ Swiggy Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Swiggy Sales Analysis** project focuses on transforming raw food delivery data into meaningful business insights using **Excel, SQL (PostgreSQL), and Python**. The project follows a complete data analytics workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, SQL-based business analysis, KPI development, and the creation of an interactive Excel dashboard. The objective is to convert raw transactional data into actionable insights that help understand business performance, customer behavior, and sales trends while supporting data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Analysis Scope

This project performs a comprehensive analysis of Swiggy sales data to gain a deeper understanding of business performance from multiple perspectives. The analysis focuses on:

- Evaluating overall sales performance across different cities and regions.
- Identifying sales trends across different days, weeks, and quarters.
- Analyzing restaurant performance based on revenue generation, customer ratings, and engagement.
- Understanding customer ordering preferences through Veg and Non-Veg sales distribution.
- Measuring category-wise contribution to overall sales and identifying high-demand food categories.
- Evaluating customer spending behaviour using Average Order Value (AOV).
- Monitoring important business KPIs to measure overall business performance.
- Identifying sales patterns, customer trends, and business opportunities through interactive visualizations.
- Generating actionable business insights and recommendations to support strategic decision-making.
